Faces of Sweden

Explore faces and costume through time with portraits in the Nordic Museum. (More info)

×

Portraits from the past can tell us a lot. What did people wear? What sort of people were painted? How did they want to be presented? Use this interactive timeline to cut through time, compare different periods and see how clothes, hairstyles and the style of portraiture changes.

‘Faces of Sweden’ showcases 785 portraits (mostly oil paintings but also prints, silhouettes, photographs) dating between 1570 - 2003 from the Nordic Museum, Stockholm.

Design/development: Olivia Vane for Swedish National Heritage Board
